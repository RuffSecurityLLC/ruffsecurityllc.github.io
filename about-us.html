How We Started

Ruff Security was founded in 2023 in Wichita, KS with the goal of providing high-quality, affordable cybersecurity services to small and medium businesses. We felt that the small business market was not properly served by the existing large cybersecurity companies.

We understand that smaller businesses have unique needs and challenges when it comes to budgets and IT staff needed to properly secure their environments. Ruff Security aims to fill that gap by offering quality services that take into consideration the limited staffing and budgets that small businesses face.

It's our belief that every business, no matter the size, should have access to security assessments, penetration testing, and other cybersecurity services to protect their data and their customers. We are committed to providing the best possible service to our clients and helping them secure their environments.
